Zafrani Pulao

by Asha Irani

2

serving

10

ingredients

30 min

to prepare

Zafrani pulao is a special sweet rice, with a combination of rich dry fruits and saffron. It's prepared on special occasions.

INGREDIENTS

2 Cup Basmati Rice
Cashewnuts (as per choice)
Almonds (as per choice)
0.5 teaspoon Green Cardamom Powder
0.25 Cup Milk
a pinch of Saffron (kesar)
4 tablespoon Ghee
0.25 teaspoon Nutmeg Powder
0.5 cup Sugar
Raisins (as per choice)
Buy all Ingredients

COOKING STEPS

Step 1 :

Wash rice twice and soak in just enough water to cover it for half an hour. Drain. Heat ghee in a deep pan and gently fry almonds, cashew-nuts and raisins.

Step 2 :

Wash rice twice and soak in just enough water to cover it for half an hour. Drain. Heat ghee in a deep pan and gently fry almonds, cashew-nuts and raisins.

Step 3 :

In the same pan add rice and sautÃ© gently for two to three minutes. Add nutmeg powder and green cardamom powder. Boil milk and sugar adding saffron.

Step 4 :

Add to rice with three and a half cups of hot water. Add raisins. Stir once. Cover and cook on low heat till done. Allow standing time of five minutes.

Step 5 : (Garnishing)

Garnish with almonds and cashew-nuts. Serve hot.
